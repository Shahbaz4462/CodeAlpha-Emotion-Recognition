How It Works

Acoustic Extraction

Raw audio is decomposed into 728 ultra-dense vectors mapping frequency, pitch, and energy boundaries.

Temporal Scaling

Data is robustly standardized, ensuring background noise and volume differences are neutralized.

3

Ensemble Inference

Three parallel machine learning trees process the feature vectors simultaneously to map emotional contours.

4

Majority Voting

The system mathematically fuses the predictions to output a final, highly confident emotional state.
